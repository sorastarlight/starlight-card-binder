User & Role Management

Owners can assign every role. Administrators may assign Moderator and Super Moderator, but cannot modify owners or administrators.

Custom role labels are display names only. Permissions always come from the mapped tier (Owner, Admin, Super Moderator, or Moderator).

Use Manage unlocks on a user to grant or revoke collector titles and avatar frames (the same unlockables from Collection Quests and the Season Pass).
