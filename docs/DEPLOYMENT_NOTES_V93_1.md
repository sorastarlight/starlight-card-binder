# V93.1 — Seamless Pack Opening & Simple Card Flip Reveal

## What changed
- The page itself becomes the blurred reveal background.
- A floating booster pack appears first and bursts open.
- Cards appear as a clean stacked deck with no framed reveal panel.
- Each card uses a simple, reliable front/back flip inspired by the supplied animation.
- Common through Epic cards use restrained glow differences.
- Legendary cards receive a stronger gold aura and circular burst while keeping the same simple flip.
- The reveal always uses the top-level browser viewport.
- The previous multi-spin reveal was removed because it could remain stuck on the card back.
