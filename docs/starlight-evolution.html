How Evolution works

Open a card in My Card Collection, then Evolve when you have enough duplicate extras. Each step spends extras and keeps one protected copy.

  1. Collect duplicate copies of a card (extras beyond your protected 1).
  2. Open that card’s Full View in Collection.
  3. Confirm Evolve — duplicates orbit, burst, and reveal a richer Evolution frame.
  4. Unfuse later if you want a partial refund of extras.
