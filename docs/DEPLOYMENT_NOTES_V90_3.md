# V90.3 — Collector Card Details Overhaul

No SQL migration is required.

Replace the deployed `docs` folder with this package and hard-refresh the browser.

## Updated collector-facing areas
- Binder selected-card panel
- Binder Full View
- My Collection cards and duplicate cards
- Checklist
- Booster and gift card reveal summaries
- Received Gifts presentation

## Display behavior
- Rarity and Category remain prominent.
- Subcategory appears when assigned.
- Variant and Finish appear when meaningful, and are fully shown in Full View.
- Distribution, availability, promo/event status, and tags are placed in expandable detail sections.
- Empty or default metadata is suppressed in compact views.
- Uncollected cards keep their richer metadata hidden.
